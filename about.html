Know Us

A decade ago, we embarked on a journey to establish an online store dedicated to selling snowboards. At that time, existing ecommerce solutions did not provide us with the level of control and customization necessary for our vision of success. Faced with this challenge, we made the decision to take matters into our own hands and built our own ecommerce platform. Our creation, Forge Fits, was born out of the need for a solution that could cater to the unique requirements of our snowboard business. Unlike existing platforms, Forge Fits was designed to offer unparalleled control and flexibility. This allowed us to shape and tailor our online store precisely to our specifications, ensuring a seamless and optimized experience for both our team and customers.

At Forge Fits, our story started with a passion for snowboards, leading us to craft a unique ecommerce solution a decade ago. Today, we empower businesses of all sizes to thrive online, in stores, and on-the-go with our versatile and customizable platform.
